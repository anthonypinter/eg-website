Ping Pong Ball Science Magic
(Full 10-Minute Routine)


This is the finale of the 2015 ASTC Live Demo Hour.  500+ people in the audience.

I used a liquid nitrogen explosion to launch 2500 white ping pong balls into the air. Mixed in were 52 orange ping pong balls, each with the name of a playing card on it.  My job was to catch the one ping pong ball printed with the card which was randomly selected by someone from the audience.
